## INFORMATION WE COLLECT FROM YOU

- **Device Information**: Hardware models, OS versions, software, file versions, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, mobile network info, installed apps, etc.
- **Account Information**: Full name, email, address, postal code, password, gender, phone number, profile picture (optional).
- **Preferences**: Time zone, language, search terms, feature usage, clicked ads.
- **Your Content**: Ratings, reviews, order history, favorites, etc.
- **Communications**: Messages between you and Swiggy, participation in surveys, contests, etc.
- **Transactional Info**: Phone number, address, email, tokenized billing info.
- **User Content (Public Posts)**: Ratings, reviews, comments, etc., posted publicly.
- **Usage Information**: Traffic data, location, logs, etc.
- **Files and Metadata**: Access with your consent.
- **Location**: Real-time location, if permitted.
- **Last URL Visited**
- **Apps on Device**: Detected presence/details of other apps.
- **From PDPs**: Photos, PAN, Aadhaar/Voter ID, DL, bank details, blood group, emergency contact, etc.

We use this information to personalize and improve our services, provide support, analyze trends, comply with legal obligations, and enhance user experience.
